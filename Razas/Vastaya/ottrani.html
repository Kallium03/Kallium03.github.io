Vastaya
Los Vastaya son una raza quimérica de Runaterra que son los descendientes mágicos más débiles de los mortales iluminados que tomaron el poder del reino espiritual conocido como Vastayashai'rei. Los Vastaya pueden verse considerablemente diferentes entre sí, pero todos comparten características animales y humanas.

Ottrani
Ability Scores: Wis +2; Con +1
Size: Medium
Speed: 35 ft.

Age. Although vastayas reach physical maturity at about the same age as humans, their understanding of adulthood goes beyond physical growth to encompass worldly experience. A vastaya can live about 800 years old.
Size. Vastayas vary widely in height and build, from barely 3 feet to well over 6 feet tall. Regardless of your position in that range, your size is Medium.
Darkvision. Accustomed to the night sky, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
Wanderer. Whenever you make a long or high jump, you can roll a d8 and add the number rolled to the number of feet you cover, even when making a standing jump. This extra distance costs movement as normal. Also, you can walk normally across difficult terrains, even while in stealth.
Calm Minded. You have advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws, and you can't be frightened.
Shared Pain. As a Reaction, you can take half the damage a creature you can see have taken instead of them. You can use this trait a number of times equal to half your level rounded up. You regain only one use of this trait on a long rest.
Tireless Precision. You are proficient in one of the following skills of your choice: Arcana, History, Investigation and Medicine. You are also proficient with one tool of your choice. Whenever you make an ability check with the chosen skill or tool, roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the check's total.
Fey Ancestry. You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
Languages. You can speak, read, and write Ottrani and undestand one extra language of your choice.
